Precalculus Equation Sheet

Lines & Circles

Concept Form
Slope-intercept \(y = mx + b\)
Point-slope \(y - y_1 = m(x - x_1)\)
Circle \((x-h)^2 + (y-k)^2 = r^2\)
Distance formula \(d = \sqrt{(x_2-x_1)^2 + (y_2-y_1)^2}\)

Quadratics

Concept Form
Standard form \(f(x) = ax^2 + bx + c\)
Vertex form \(f(x) = a(x-h)^2 + k\)
Vertex (from standard form) \(x = -\dfrac{b}{2a}\)

Exponential & Logarithmic

Concept Form
Exponential growth/decay \(f(t) = a \cdot b^t\)
Logarithm definition \(\log_b(x) = y \iff b^y = x\)
Product rule \(\log_b(xy) = \log_b(x) + \log_b(y)\)
Quotient rule \(\log_b(x/y) = \log_b(x) - \log_b(y)\)
Power rule \(\log_b(x^n) = n\log_b(x)\)

Rational Functions & Angular Measure

Concept Form
Degrees to radians \(\text{radians} = \text{degrees} \times \dfrac{\pi}{180}\)
Arc length \(s = r\theta\)

Trigonometry & Sinusoidal Functions

Concept Form
Sinusoidal function \(f(t) = A\sin\big(B(t-C)\big) + D\)
Amplitude \(A\)
Period \(\dfrac{2\pi}{B}\)
Phase shift \(C\)
Vertical shift \(D\)
Pythagorean identity \(\sin^2(\theta) + \cos^2(\theta) = 1\)
